Rules & Scoring

🔧

Each robot must be autonomous, with a maximum size of 15x15 cm.

🏠

The maze has a 10x10 sq. feet area with checkpoints and dead-ends.

🔴

Scoring Criteria:

  • Fastest time with fewest penalties wins.
  • Penalties for wall collisions, resets, boundary exits, and missed checkpoints.
